About Us (marami kami)

Capturing the world through a lens is just the start; for me, it’s about the narrative that lives within the frame. As a photographer and writer, I find beauty in the intersection of visual and verbal storytelling. Whether I’m chasing the perfect golden hour light to capture a candid moment or sitting down to weave those observations into prose, my goal is always to document the human experience in its most authentic form.

When I’m not behind a camera or a keyboard, you’ll likely find me immersed in a digital landscape. Gaming isn't just a hobby, it’s a source of inspiration. The intricate world-building and character arcs found in modern titles often spark new ideas for my own creative projects. This passion for interactive media keeps my perspective fresh and fuels my drive to explore how different forms of media can evoke emotion and build community.

Balancing these creative pursuits with the rigors of being a student keeps me grounded and constantly evolving. My academic life provides the discipline and critical thinking necessary to refine my crafts, while my hobbies offer a much-needed outlet for self-expression. I’m a perpetual learner, always looking for the next skill to master, the next story to tell, and the next level to unlock.
